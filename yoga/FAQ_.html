What is different about Yoga Peace?

At Yoga Peace we try to help people feel off the mat what they come to feel on the mat. we work hard to create a place where peopel can really explore and come to know themselves. we do this by focusing on meditation rather than the physical practise.

What are your times?

We have yoga classes from 9am-9pm evry day exclusding Sunday

What is Yoga Peace Group?

Yoga peace is a way we can create a team effort in Yoga, it might seem its just you on the mat however with yoga peace we try to bring the experiences of teh mat home with you.
